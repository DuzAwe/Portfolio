Drop Dead Comedians

Drop Dead Comedians was a personal project of mine. Its built on Wordpress and a modified theme. The entire focus of this design reflected the game news world. No one cares about day old news so the front page would only display news from the current day. You could stick posts to the front page if you wanted to feature it for longer.

The logo was based on the comic series that launched the site original.
