Dylan Thomas - Little Machine

This video was commissioned by the Welsh Council to celebrate Dylan Thomas's Centenary. The project was to show Dylan's swansea emphasizing the places special to him and his fans. It was to feature the Dunvant Male Choir as a requirement. It was completed in 4 weeks and presented with two different cuts. One with the choir and one without. The team felt that the quire ruined the overall message of the video so we made a second option incase the client wasn't happy with the final product they requested. Above is the cut I felt happiest with the other version is available here.
