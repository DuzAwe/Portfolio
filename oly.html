Olympics 2012 Poster Design

The posters for the 2012 olympics were awful. This was my alternative to what they used.

I felt that using identifiable landmarks from London better equated to the campaign message. The pop culture of the world is infatuated with Harry Potter and Doctor Who. So now ever one knows what Big Ben or the London eye is.

As for the colour choice I made this was right at the start of the Windows Metro it may even be slightly before it. But I felt that a flat colour pallet was far more enjoyable for the eyes than the unholy lens flairing 90's style that was the actual campaign.
